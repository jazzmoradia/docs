Help is close, human, and searchable. Whether you've hit a setup snag, want to share what you've built, or just have a quick question, there's a channel that fits — and you don't have to dig through a forum or wait days for a reply. Here's where to find what you need.

## Support thread — best for bugs and setup

The primary support channel is the **gist comments thread**. It's the fastest way to get a real answer, and every reply stays permanently searchable so the next person with the same question finds it too.

The thread is monitored continuously — including evenings and weekends. Post your question with a brief description of what you're seeing and what you've tried, and you'll typically hear back quickly.

**[Open the support thread →](https://gist.github.com/jazzmoradia/6c4383fb51914f9bf083ccd472ee6b12)**

<Note>
  Before leaving a frustrated review on Fab, try posting here first. The vast majority of issues turn out to be save flow, collision, or a single setting — and most are resolved in one or two replies.
</Note>

## AI docs assistant — instant answers

OmniScape includes a **NotebookLM-powered docs assistant** trained on the full OmniScape documentation and codebase. It's the fastest way to get answers to setup and troubleshooting questions without waiting for a human reply.

**How to open it:**
- Click the **note icon** in the Control Center title bar, or
- Go to **Window → OmniScape** — you'll see a prompt to open the assistant on first launch.

The assistant is great for "how do I…" questions, explaining settings, and walking through troubleshooting steps. For bugs that look like genuine issues, follow up on the support thread so they can be tracked.

## Discord — showcase, suggestions, and early chatter

The OmniScape Discord is an optional space for the community side of things: **feature requests, suggestions, showcases**, and early chatter about upcoming updates. It's a great place to share screenshots of your settlements or pick up ideas from what others are building.

Discord is **not required** to use OmniScape — it's there when you want it, not a gatekeeping requirement.

**[Join the Discord →](https://discord.gg/UjXbjW6gUH)**

You can also open Discord directly from the **COMMUNITY** button in the Control Center title bar, or via **Window → OmniScape**.

## Fab — purchases, downloads, and listing updates

Fab is where OmniScape lives for purchasing, downloading updates, and reading listing-level information. When a new version is released, you'll update through the Epic Games Launcher from your Fab library.

**[OmniScape on Fab →](https://fab.com/s/4aeff49faf04)**

<Note>
  Fab is not the right place for bug reports or setup questions — the review section isn't a support channel and answers don't stay searchable there. Use the gist thread for anything technical.
</Note>
