OmniScape is a procedural settlement generator for Unreal Engine 5.5+ that turns your existing meshes and Blueprints into complete, believable towns, fortresses, and villages — laid out, streeted, walled, and detailed from a single actor and a single seed. You stay in control of the art; OmniScape handles everything about how that art is arranged.

## How it works

OmniScape follows a straightforward handshake between your art and its layout engine. Understanding these three points covers most of what the tool does.

<Steps>
  <Step title="You bring the art">
    Assign your Static Meshes or Blueprint classes to the **Large**, **Medium**, and **Small** building tiers in the Build tab. Add road meshes, wall pieces, flora, and props as needed — or leave any layer empty to skip it entirely.
  </Step>
  <Step title="OmniScape handles the layout">
    One click on **Generate** and OmniScape places zones, roads, buildings, walls, flora, fauna, and ambient audio across the settlement footprint. Five layout patterns, three road elevation modes, and a full wall system give you broad creative range without manual placement.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Same seed, same result">
    Every machine that runs the same seed and settings produces the exact same layout. Hand the level to a collaborator, re-generate after changing a mesh, or version the seed in source control — the outcome is always deterministic.
  </Step>
</Steps>
