OmniScape turns one actor into a complete settlement. You place an **OmniScape Generator**, open the **Control Center**, assign your meshes and Blueprints, and generate — streets, buildings, walls, and natural detail compose around a single seed. Change a setting, generate again, and the result is always consistent.

It is built for level designers and environment artists who want the speed of procedural generation with the readability of hand-placed work — and none of the node-graph overhead.

## The mental model

The Control Center is organised the way you think about a place — four tabs, in workflow order, each answering one question. Tab order never changes.

## What makes it different

- **Deterministic seed stability** — lock a composition and trust it through ship.
- **BYOA** — any art style via your own meshes and Blueprints.
- **Single-panel UX** — no PCG graph required.
- **Layout intelligence** — multiple patterns, road hierarchy, and concave-hull walls.
- **Hand-placed quality** — spatial rhythm that reads convincingly from the street and from above.
