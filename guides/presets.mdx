A preset is a **parameter snapshot** — settlement config, pattern tuning, roads, walls, building counts, flora, zone distribution, and districts. Load a baseline, assign your art, then **Generate**.

<Note>
  Presets store **numbers and toggles**, never your mesh lists. Volumes, anchors, road asset lists, and wall mesh lists also stay out — assign your kit before or after loading.
</Note>

## Using presets

From the **preset row** in the Control Center you can load, save, update, rename, and delete.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Load a baseline">
    Pick an archetype from the dropdown. Built-in presets open with the current road stack (Legacy off) so everyone targets the same baseline.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign your meshes">
    Drop your buildings, roads, walls, and environment kits into the tabs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate, then tune">
    Generate, then adjust. **Baseline revert** restores the state from when you opened the Generator.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## The twelve archetypes <span className="os-tag os-phase-layout">v3.0</span>

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Fortified & defence">
    | Preset | Character |
    |---|---|
    | **Citadel and Keep** | Concentric rings, angular curtain wall, ten towers, military keep core. |
    | **Medieval Walled Town** | Dense walled old town — market, residential, and sacred zones; organic curtain wall. |
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Urban & planned">
    | Preset | Character |
    |---|---|
    | **Urban District Blocks** | Open Voronoi city blocks — civic, commercial, and industrial districts. |
    | **Colonial Grid Town** | Compact parallel-grid town — civic core, residential blocks. |
    | **Metropolitan Grid** | Large-scale axial grid — 22 nodes, four zone types, high sprawl. |
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Rural & county">
    | Preset | Character |
    |---|---|
    | **Quiet Hamlet** | A tiny three-node organic cluster — an intimate rural vignette. |
    | **Farm Country** | Wide scatter hamlets — agricultural, residential, and market zones. |
    | **Coastal Fishing Village** | Harbor-front organic village — irregular roadside frontage. |
    | **Desert Trading Post** | Sparse crossroads post — market + residential, worn natural tracks. |
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Hills & landmark">
    | Preset | Character |
    |---|---|
    | **Ridge Village** | Hillside terrace hamlet — contour roads, sacred + market nodes. |
    | **Ruined Hillside Enclave** | Sparse ruined perimeter on a slope — low density, partial organic wall. |
    | **Sacred Temple Precinct** | Ceremonial radial — three nodes, sacred core, curved approach roads. |
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Warning>
  **Empty preset list after install?** Reinstall or update from [Fab](https://fab.com/s/4aeff49faf04) so the full package — including built-in presets — is present.
</Warning>

<Tip>
  Built-in presets are tuned for the current road stack. The **intersection mesh** isn't stored inside presets — assign it after loading.
</Tip>
