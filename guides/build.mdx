<span className="os-tag os-phase-build">Build · What?</span>

Build is where your art lives. Assign meshes or Blueprints per tier; OmniScape places them with hand-built rhythm. Presets store the numbers — you bring the kit.

## Tiers

| Tier | Role |
|---|---|
| **Large** | One landmark **per node** — the silhouette. Required for anything to spawn. |
| **Medium** | The main fabric around nodes. |
| **Small** | Filler and edges. |
| **Sprawl** | Gap-fill controlled by **Edge Density**. |

**Weights** are relative frequency — ratios matter more than raw numbers. A 10 vs 1 weighting just means 10:1 odds within that tier.

## Bring your own art

Each asset slot accepts one of:

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Static mesh" icon="box">
    Instanced where possible — fast and light. The default for most buildings.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Blueprint" icon="code">
    A full actor when you need logic, interaction, or spawned children.
  </Card>
</Columns>

Per slot you can also set a **Bounds override** (for a bad pivot or bounds) and a **Spur street slot** for driveway-style frontage.

<Note>
  When both a Blueprint and a mesh are present on a chosen entry, the **Blueprint wins**; the mesh is the fallback.
</Note>

## Placement

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Cluster (default)">
    Rings of samples around each node. Reads well for organic cores and hillside clusters. Per-anchor medium/small overrides apply in Cluster mode.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Roadside">
    Plots along roads with variable **frontage**, **depth**, and **variation**; buildings face the street. Roadside uses the **global** medium/small lists only.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

Per tier you also get **scale**, **max per node** (medium/small), and an optional **face toward nearest road** toggle.

## Sprawl — Edge Density

**Build → Sprawl → Edge Density** (0 = off) fills the gaps between main clusters and roads with small buildings, respecting the road network so sprawl stays cohesive. This is the artist-facing sprawl control.

| Control | Range | Default |
|---|---|---|
| Medium per node | 0 – 100 | 8 |
| Small per node | 0 – 200 | 16 |
| Large / medium / small scale | 0.1 – 10 | 1 |
| Edge density | 0 – 1 | 0 (off) |

<Tip>
  Density feel as a rule of thumb — **Sparse:** 2–6 medium / 4–12 small per node. **Typical:** 6–12 / 12–24. **Busy:** 12–30 / 24–80.
</Tip>
