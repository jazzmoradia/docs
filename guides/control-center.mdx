Everything in OmniScape routes through one dockable panel. It's organised in workflow order, top to bottom and left to right — so the panel reads like the act of building a place.

## Explore the panel

Click the tabs below. The preview re-emphasises the layer each tab owns, and the reading panel maps each capability to where it lives.

## Anatomy, top to bottom

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Title bar — branding, community, actor selector" icon="panel-top">
    The hexagon mark and **OmniScape** wordmark, a **developer note** (updates and the docs assistant), a **Community** link to Discord, and an **actor selector** on the right when more than one Generator exists in the level.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Action toolbar — Generate, Save to Level, Clear" icon="play">
    **Generate** runs the full pipeline (it clears first). **Save to Level** persists the result. **Clear** removes generated content before a full redo.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Preset row — parameter snapshots" icon="bookmark">
    Load, save, update, rename, or delete a [preset](/guides/presets). Presets store numbers and toggles — not your mesh lists. **Auto-Generate** regenerates on every change (heavy).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Live preview — the 2D map" icon="map">
    A pannable, zoomable map of nodes, roads, and walls with a colour-coded legend. It updates shortly after you change settings and stays in sync with undo/redo. The canvas edges hold quick tools for **manual anchors**, **spline volumes**, the **seed**, and the **Atlas** fold-corner.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Tab bar — Layout, Build, Structure, Ambient" icon="layout-panel-left">
    The four workflow tabs. Tab order is workflow order and never changes. The canvas always sits above the tabs.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Status bar — state, counts, Stable/Beta" icon="panel-bottom">
    Generation state, live **L / M / S** building and **road** counts, last generate time, the **Stable / Beta** switch, and the engine badge.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Stable vs Beta

The status bar (bottom-right) shows the current mode.

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Stable (default)" icon="circle-check">
    Everything you need for a complete settlement: layout, roads, walls, buildings, flora, and zones.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Beta" icon="flask-conical">
    Switch to unlock five extra pillars — **Fauna**, **Ambient Audio**, **Landscape Bake**, **PCG Hand-off**, and road landscape deform.
  </Card>
</Columns>

<Tip>
  **Live Preview is not Auto-Generate.** The preview is a lightweight map; Auto respawns full geometry on every edit. Tune with the preview, then **Generate** when ready.
</Tip>

<Note>
  Day-to-day, high-leverage controls live in the Control Center. Power-user, diagnostic, and experimental fields live on the actor's **Details** panel.
</Note>
